About Me

I'm Mijal Maharjan, 22 years old, and I really love gaming, watching anime and movies, and listening to music. Games let me be whoever I want, movies and anime tell awesome stories, and music keeps me company all day. I enjoy diving into digital adventures where every click leads to something new and exciting.

Arriving in Australia from Nepal was a leap into the unknown, but it's been a journey filled with achievements. Becoming independent and earning for myself has been incredibly empowering. Securing a job in the hospitality sector was a significant milestone, reflecting my dedication and adaptability in a new country. One of my proudest achievements has been making friends from diverse backgrounds. Connecting with people from various cultures has broadened my perspective and enriched my life in ways I never imagined. These achievements fuel my determination to continue thriving in this new chapter of my life.

Looking ahead, my goals for the future are crystal clear and deeply motivating. Graduating with a decent score is not just about earning a piece of paper; it's a testament to my hard work and dedication. My ultimate aim is to find a job in the IT sector, where I can apply my skills and knowledge to make a meaningful impact. Alongside this, I aspire to earn enough money to support myself comfortably and achieve financial stability. Owning a car is more than just a convenience; it represents a milestone of independence and mobility. But above all, one of my greatest aspirations is to make my parents proud. Their unwavering support and sacrifices have fueled my journey, and I'm determined to repay their faith by achieving success and happiness in all that I do.
