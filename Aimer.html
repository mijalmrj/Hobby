Aimer

Aimer, often stylized as AIMer, is the stage name of a Japanese singer and songwriter known for her distinctive voice, emotive performances, and versatile musical style. Born on July 9, 1990, in Kumamoto Prefecture, Japan, her real name is unknown as she prefers to keep her personal life private.
Aimer's musical career began in 2011 when she made her major debut under the label Defstar Records with the single "Rokutousei no Yoru," which was featured as the ending theme for the anime series "No. 6." Since then, she has released numerous singles, albums, and collaborations, establishing herself as one of Japan's most acclaimed vocalists.
Her music spans various genres, including pop, rock, R&B, jazz, and electronica, showcasing her versatility as an artist. Aimer's songs often feature heartfelt lyrics and melodies that resonate with listeners, earning her a dedicated fan base both in Japan and internationally.
A notable aspect of Aimer's career is her collaboration with various prominent artists and composers within the Japanese music industry. She has worked with renowned producers such as Hiroyuki Sawano, TK from Ling tosite sigure, and Yoko Kanno, among others, further expanding her musical horizons and pushing artistic boundaries.
Aimer's discography includes several successful albums such as "Sleepless Nights," "Midnight Sun," "Daydream," and "Walpurgis," each showcasing her evolving style and growth as an artist. She has also contributed music to various anime series, films, and commercials, further solidifying her presence in the Japanese music scene.
Known for her captivating live performances, Aimer has held numerous concerts and tours across Japan, as well as overseas performances in countries such as France and Taiwan. Her powerful vocals and emotional delivery leave a lasting impression on audiences, garnering praise from fans and critics alike.
Overall, Aimer continues to captivate audiences with her soulful voice, heartfelt music, and dynamic performances, solidifying her position as one of Japan's most beloved and respected singers in contemporary music.
